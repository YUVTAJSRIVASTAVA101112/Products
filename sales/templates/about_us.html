ABOUT US

Welcome to SANCHVI STUDIO, your number one source for all things fashion. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of our products, with a focus on quality, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2019 by Sanchvi, SANCHVI STUDIO has come a long way from its beginnings in Bengaluru. When Sanchvi first started out, her passion for eco-friendly fashion drove her to quit her day job and turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky, eco-friendly wing of the fashion industry.

We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
